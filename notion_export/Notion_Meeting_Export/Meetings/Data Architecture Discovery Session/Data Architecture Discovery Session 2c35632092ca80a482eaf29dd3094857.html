⚠️ Data Quality Challenges

Manual vs. Automated Entry

  • PLC-driven measurements are exact (down to 0.00)
  • Manual entries often have operators entering expected values, not actual
    • Example: Recipe calls for 20,600 lbs, they put in exactly 20,600 — "amazing accuracy"
  • Makes traceability and analysis challenging
  • Good PLC data enables bad batch investigation

System Variations

  • Three different ways of doing things across facilities
  • Need for data domain restructuring
  • Goal: "One lake" database/interface for reporting

🔗 Key Data Flows

MES → M3

  • Primarily for inventory management
  • Does NOT update costing or formulation details
  • Reduces raw material inventory based on consumption
  • Increases finished goods inventory based on production

Batch Traceability

  • Each batch has a GUID tied to specific plant formula
  • Batch records link to product/formulation IDs from M3
  • Full traceability from lowest granularity to higher hierarchies ✅
