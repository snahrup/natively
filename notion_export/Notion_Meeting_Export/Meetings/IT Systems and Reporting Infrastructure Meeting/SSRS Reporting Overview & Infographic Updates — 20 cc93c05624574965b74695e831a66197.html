Architecture Insights

  • M3 cloud and on-prem schemas almost identical; many Interplastic reports reused
  • ETQ receives data via direct DB loads from M3, not through ION/BODs
  • IDM hyperlinks currently point to on-prem paths; no known durable GUID
  • DNS entries used for DB hosts to allow future moves, but not a full link-resiliency solution
  • Some reports use link servers across systems, raising potential breakage risk
